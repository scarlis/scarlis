Emergency Response Training Module

Scarlis Education’s Emergency Response Module helps businesses improve preparedness through a structured, evidence-based approach. Our module includes 30 carefully designed questions covering 3 key components of emergency preparedness:

  • Community Preparedness
  • Occupational Preparedness
  • Effective Emergency Response

With an easy-to-use interface, this time-efficient module maximizes compliance and focuses on outcomes that make a real impact. The program also integrates image-based questions to help identify risks in different scenarios.

The module aligns closely with Australian Standards and was developed in collaboration with DFES and SES.

Key Features

Evidence-Based Approach

The module is built on proven strategies to ensure your staff is equipped to handle emergency situations effectively.

Time-Efficient & Engaging

Short but comprehensive, this training maximizes compliance by delivering high-impact knowledge in a manageable format.

Certification & Validation

Upon completion, participants receive a certified completion certificate for their records and validation. Location stamps ensure authenticity.

How the Training Works

Our training is designed to be simple and user-friendly. Participants are presented with a series of 30 questions divided into 3 sections. Here's what you can expect:

  1. Each section contains 10 questions focused on community and occupational preparedness.
  2. Some questions feature images where participants must identify risks by clicking on the part of the image showing the hazard.
  3. Participants must answer all questions correctly to proceed to the next section. Incorrect answers must be revisited until corrected.
  4. Once all sections are completed correctly, a certificate will be issued with the participant’s name, date, and location information.
